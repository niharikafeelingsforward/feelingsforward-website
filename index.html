About Me

Hi, I'm Niharika Srivastava

I'm a certified Mental Health Professional with over 7 years of experience working with adolescents and adults. I started Feelings Forward during the Covid-19 pandemic because I believe mental health conversations should be open, accessible, and free from stigma.
