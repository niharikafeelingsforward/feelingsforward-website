My Story

Niharika Srivastava

From a young age, I felt a strong inclination to support and understand others. After exploring different paths, I found my calling in mental health and became a certified Mental Health Professional in 2017 — a journey that continues to be deeply meaningful.

Over the years, I have developed a diverse range of experience working with adolescents and adults navigating a wide spectrum of psychological concerns. My work is grounded in a multicultural, trauma-informed lens that recognises the impact of socio-cultural contexts, relational dynamics, and lived experiences on emotional well-being. I draw from an integrative approach, emphasising safety, attunement, and collaborative exploration within the therapeutic space.

I strive to create a space that is empathetic, non-judgmental, and holding — where clients can explore their inner world, build self-awareness, and move toward greater emotional regulation, resilience, and confidence.

I founded Feelings Forward as a platform rooted in the belief that growth is an ongoing, evolving process. It is a space dedicated to encouraging reflection, fostering emotional insight, and supporting individuals in leading more grounded and fulfilling lives.

My work is guided by a commitment to spreading awareness, reducing stigma, and advocating for mental health with sensitivity, inclusivity, and care.

My Approach

How I Work With You

I believe therapy works best when it feels like a genuine collaboration. I don't work on you — I work with you. My approach is warm, reflective, and grounded in creating a space where you feel safe to explore even the most difficult parts of yourself.

I use guided questions and structured frameworks to help you build insight and emotional awareness, moving at a pace that feels right for you. Sessions are boundaried but human — professional in structure, personal in spirit.

Whether you're in crisis, navigating a transition, or simply wanting to understand yourself better, I meet you exactly where you are.

🔒 Confidentiality

I adhere to a strict code of confidentiality and respect the privacy of your thoughts, feelings, and life narrative as stipulated by the Code of Ethics. The notable exceptions are if you share information that may cause harm to yourself or others, involves legal situations, child protection, or serious crimes.
