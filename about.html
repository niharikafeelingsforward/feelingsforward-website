My Story

Niharika Srivastava

From a young age, I knew I wanted to help others. After exploring various careers, I finally found my calling when I became a certified Mental Health Professional in 2017 — and I haven't looked back since.

Over the years, I've gathered a diversified background of experience working with adolescents and adults across a range of psychological vulnerabilities. I provide a comprehensive set of services to help boost your confidence and offer support as you navigate your personal health and wellness challenges.

I started Feelings Forward as an Instagram handle during the Covid-19 pandemic because I believe that growing and evolving to lead fuller, more wholesome lives is key to holistic well-being. I strive to support my clients in exploring the depths of their inner world in an empathetic, caring, safe, and holding space.

My aim is to disseminate information, raise awareness, de-stigmatise, and advocate for mental health needs.

My Approach

How I Work With You

I believe therapy works best when it feels like a genuine collaboration. I don't work on you — I work with you. My approach is warm, reflective, and grounded in creating a space where you feel safe to explore even the most difficult parts of yourself.

I use guided questions and structured frameworks to help you build insight and emotional awareness, moving at a pace that feels right for you. Sessions are boundaried but human — professional in structure, personal in spirit.

Whether you're in crisis, navigating a transition, or simply wanting to understand yourself better, I meet you exactly where you are.

🔒 Confidentiality

I adhere to a strict code of confidentiality and respect the privacy of your thoughts, feelings, and life narrative as stipulated by the Code of Ethics. The notable exceptions are if you share information that may cause harm to yourself or others, involves legal situations, child protection, or serious crimes.
